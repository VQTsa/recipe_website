### Ingredients
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1 teaspoon fish sauce
- 1 tablespoon mild curry powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1 pound thawed peeled deveined raw large shrimp
- 1 sweet red pepper diced
- 2 green onions chopped
- 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
- 4 lime wedges
