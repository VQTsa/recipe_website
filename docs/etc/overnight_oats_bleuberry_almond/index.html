Overnight Blueberry Almond Oats

Published Nov 11, 2019 by in Other at https://patricerecipebook.com/etc/overnight_oats_bleuberry_almond/

Ingredients

  • 3⁄4 cup large flake oats (yellow Quaker Oats brand bag)
  • 1 tbsp ground flax
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or brown sugar
  • 1⁄4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tbsp chopped roasted almonds
  • 3⁄4 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup almond or regular milk (we find almond milk adds a nutty flavour)

Directions

Combine all ingredients, mix, cover and let sit in the fridge overnight Enjoy in the morning! You can eat it cold or heat it up a bit in the microwave
