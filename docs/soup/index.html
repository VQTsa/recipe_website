Carrot ginger soup

Ingredients 3 lb peeled carrots Olive oil Salt and pepper 1 peeled, cored apple cut into thin slices 1 onion, diced 2 cloves of garlic, pressed ~ 2-4 tablespoons of chopped fresh ginger (can add more if you like the flavor) 1 tsp ground coriander 1 tsp all spice 4-7 cups of vegetable broth Half-and-half cream (optional) Fresh cilantro (optional) Directions Roast carrots at 425F for 45 minutes on a baking sheet with olive oil, salt and pepper, turning over half way.
